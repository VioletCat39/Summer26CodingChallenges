Password Generator Challenge:
Now that the password security checker has been created, let's make a password generator to generate a random and secure password!

For this challenge, I am outlining the following parameters:
The password must be 12 to 24 characters in length (length determined by either input or random number generation)
The password must have one uppercase, one lowercase, one number, and one special character.
No character must repeat one after another (ie, no two characters in a row are the same).
